Aaron Ansuini

Summary

Aaron Ansuini is a trans & disabled social justice activist and a pre-service teacher at Concordia University in the Art Education Department. He founded the non-proﬁt organization “Let’s Get By Together” in 2018, which uses art as creative activism to raise awareness and provide resources directly to the Queer community. He also creates online educational content on themes ranging from gender identity, sexuality, disability, mental health, and art, which can be found on his social media platforms.
